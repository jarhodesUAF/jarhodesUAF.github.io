Research:

For the last few years, my primary interest has been the mathematical framework underlying phylogenetics — the inference of evolutionary trees from biological data such as genetic sequences. Much of my recent work has focused on using algebraic perspectives to understand probabilistic models of sequence evolution. This has led to more general algebraic work on statistical models with discrete random variables, and to work on questions of tensor rank.
